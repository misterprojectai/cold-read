# QUICK-START

**For Jordan:** 44, two years out of a long relationship, a few apps, a few first dates, no idea why it keeps ending the same way. Five steps. Under five minutes. No need to read anything else first.

---

**Step 1 — Create a Claude.ai Project.**

Go to [claude.ai](https://claude.ai). Click **Projects** in the left sidebar, then **New Project**. Name it anything — "Cold Read" works.

---

**Step 2 — Add the files and instructions.**

Inside your project, upload the files from this repo: `rules.md`, `identity.md`, `examples.md`, `coach-playbook.md`, and the four files in the `reference/` folder (`connection-triangle.md`, `spark-formula.md`, `response-tools.md`, `playfulness-ladder.md`).

Then paste the project instructions from `project-instructions.md` into the **Instructions** box at the top of the project. This is what tells the system how to behave in voice mode — without it, the files are knowledge but the coaching loop does not activate.

---

**Step 3 — Open a voice conversation and say hi.**

Click **New Chat** inside the project. Switch to **voice mode** (tap the microphone icon). Say hi. The coach presents six exercises — practice a live conversation, run drills, debrief something that happened, prep for something coming up, work through a freeze, or daily narration.

---

**Step 4 — Pick one.**

Say "one" to practice a live conversation. The coach asks who you want to talk to — Maya (warm, easy), Nadia (cool, deliberate), or Sera (high energy, direct) — and then where: coffee shop, bar, park, public transportation, or through a friend. Pick both, and you are talking to her. Say whatever comes to mind. When you want feedback, say "coach." The system switches to Cold Read, runs the diagnostic, gives one correction, and asks if you want to go again.

Pick any other option and the coach takes it from there — you describe the situation, the coach diagnoses.

---

**Step 5 — Or start with the coach directly.**

If you already have a real situation to work through — a freeze, a date that went wrong, an approach you are preparing for — skip the practice characters and talk to the coach directly. Say "coach" at the start, or just describe what happened. The system detects the session mode automatically and coaches from there.

Try this as your first message if you want to feel how the coaching works before practicing with Maya:

> I was at the grocery store this morning. A woman made eye contact with me twice. The second time she smiled. I grabbed my stuff and left. I've been thinking about it all day and I don't know what's wrong with me.

The session ends when you name a specific commitment — a named place, a named time, a named behavior. That commitment is the only thing the session is trying to produce.
